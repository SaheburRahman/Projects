Explore my portfolio of projects, where creativity meets engineering. From robotics to custom electronics designs, each project reflects my passion for innovation and problem-solving.
<h1>High Frequency Tesla Coil</h1>



<h2>Description</h2>
A high-frequency Tesla coil designed for efficient wireless energy transfer and high-voltage experiments. This project showcases expertise in resonant circuit design, high-frequency switching, and power electronics, with applications in education, research, and entertainment.

<h2>Equipments and Tools Used</h2>

- <b>Magnetic Wire</b>
- <b>Resistor, capacitor and other passive components</b>
